# I'm Buying in NSW for $800,000. How Much Stamp Duty Will I Pay?

Before you make an offer, model stamp duty alongside your deposit and lender costs. Stamp duty changes by state, property value, and concession eligibility.

Use the Stamp Duty Calculator above, switch the state dropdown, and test your target purchase price before you commit.

First home buyer concessions can materially change the result. Treat this as an estimate and confirm with your state revenue office or conveyancer before signing.
