# Can I Afford an Investment Property on a $120,000 Salary?

Borrowing capacity starts with income, expenses, dependants, and existing debts. It does not end there.

Use the Borrowing Capacity Calculator to estimate the upper limit, then use Rental Yield to see whether the property makes sense as an investment.

A lender may offer more than you feel comfortable borrowing. Keep a cash buffer for vacancy, repairs, rate rises, and insurance.
