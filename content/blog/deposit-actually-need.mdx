# How Much Deposit Do I Actually Need for a $800,000 Property?

Your deposit is not the whole cash requirement. You also need stamp duty, conveyancing, inspections, lender fees, moving costs, and a buffer.

The LVR calculator shows whether your deposit keeps you under 80% LVR. The stamp duty calculator then shows the extra cash needed at settlement.

If your LVR is above 80%, lenders mortgage insurance may apply. That can change whether a smaller deposit is still worth it.
