# Selling My Investment Property - What Tax Will I Actually Owe?

Capital gains tax depends on your sale price, cost base, improvements, selling costs, ownership structure, and whether you held the property for more than 12 months.

The CGT Calculator estimates your gross gain, discount, taxable gain, and tax payable. If the sale produces a loss, no CGT is payable on that sale.

Always speak with a tax professional before selling. Timing, income, and record keeping can change the result.
