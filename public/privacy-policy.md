## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use AusPropertyCalc:

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out via [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).

We use cookies for analytics (PostHog) and advertising (Google AdSense). By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of cookies. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 12 months. We do not sell your data to third parties.
